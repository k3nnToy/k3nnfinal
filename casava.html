Cassava Cake

Cassava Cake is a traditional Filipino dessert made from grated cassava (kamoteng kahoy), coconut milk, condensed milk, evaporated milk, eggs, and sometimes cheese or custard topping. It is typically baked until firm and golden, with a rich, chewy texture and sweet, coconut-infused flavor. The topping may be creamy and slightly caramelized, making it even more indulgent.
